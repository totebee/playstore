Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

How can I download your apps?

All our applications are available on the Google Play Store. Simply search for "Grove Tools" or the specific app name you're looking for, and you'll find our official apps. Make sure to check for our developer name to ensure you're downloading the authentic version.

Are your apps free to download?

We offer both free and premium applications. Our free apps may include in-app purchases or ads to support development, while our premium apps are ad-free and offer enhanced features. Details about pricing and available features are clearly listed on each app's Play Store page.

Do your apps collect my personal data?

We take your privacy seriously. Our apps only collect data necessary for their functionality and to improve user experience. We never sell personal information to third parties. For detailed information, please refer to the Privacy Policy for each specific application, which can be found on its Google Play Store listing.

Subscription & Payments

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

You can manage your subscriptions directly through the Google Play Store:

  1. Open the Google Play Store app
  2. Tap on your profile icon in the top right
  3. Select "Payments & subscriptions"
  4. Tap "Subscriptions"
  5. Find and select our app
  6. Choose "Cancel subscription" or manage your payment options
Will I receive a refund if I cancel my subscription?

When you cancel a subscription, you'll continue to have access to the premium features until the end of your current billing period. Google Play Store generally doesn't provide partial refunds for canceled subscriptions. However, if you've experienced technical issues or believe you have grounds for a refund, please contact our support team at hi@grovetools.live with details of your situation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all payment methods supported by the Google Play Store, including credit/debit cards, Google Play balance, PayPal (in supported countries), and carrier billing (where available). Payment processing is handled securely by Google, and we never have access to your complete financial information.

Technical Support

My app is crashing or not working properly. What should I do?

If you're experiencing technical issues with any of our apps, please try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Ensure your app is updated to the latest version
  2. Restart your device
  3. Check if your device meets the minimum requirements
  4. Clear the app cache (Settings > Apps > [App Name] > Storage > Clear Cache)
  5. If problems persist, please contact our support team with details about your device model, Android version, and a description of the issue
How do I transfer my app data to a new device?

Most of our apps synchronize data through your Google account. To ensure your data transfers to a new device:

  1. Make sure you're using the same Google account on both devices
  2. Enable backup in your app settings (if available)
  3. On your new device, install the app and sign in with the same Google account
  4. Allow the app to restore backed-up data when prompted

For apps that don't support automatic backup, please check the specific app's settings for export/import options or contact our support team for assistance.

Do your apps work offline?

Many of our apps offer offline functionality, allowing you to use core features without an internet connection. However, certain features like cloud sync, online content, or real-time data may require an active internet connection. Each app's description on the Play Store specifies its offline capabilities.
